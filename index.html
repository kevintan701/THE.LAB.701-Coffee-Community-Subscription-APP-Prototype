Project Overview

Project Overview

A high-fidelity prototype for a digital coffee community and subscription app that connects coffee enthusiasts with premium coffee products and like-minded individuals. This app offers a seamless experience for discovering new coffee varieties, managing subscriptions, and engaging with a passionate coffee community.

Vision & Purpose

THE.LAB.701 Coffee Community app aims to revolutionize how coffee lovers discover, purchase, and experience specialty coffee. In a market saturated with generic coffee offerings, our platform creates a curated ecosystem where quality, sustainability, and community converge. We're not just selling coffee—we're building a digital third space where the rituals and pleasures of coffee can be shared and celebrated.

Community

Connect with like-minded coffee enthusiasts, share brewing techniques, and discover new coffee varieties through a vibrant community of passionate individuals.

  • Share your coffee experiences
  • Learn from expert baristas
  • Join discussions and events

Subscription

Never run out of your favorite coffee with our flexible subscription system tailored to your preferences and schedule.

Discovery

Explore coffee knowledge, find local coffee shops, and discover upcoming events in the coffee community.

Shop

Shop for coffee beans and equipment from our official store and partner coffee shops around the country.

Key Features
Coffee Exploration & Discovery
Find coffee shops near you, learn about coffee brewing techniques, and discover coffee events in your community.
Dedicated Shopping Experience
Browse and purchase coffee beans and equipment from our official store and partner coffee shops across the country.
Flexible Subscription System
Customize delivery frequency, quantity, and grind options for regular coffee deliveries. Easily pause, skip, or modify your subscription anytime.
Community Engagement
Share brewing techniques, rate products, and connect with other coffee enthusiasts. Post your coffee experiences and follow other users.
Seamless Ordering
Easy checkout process with saved payment methods and shipping addresses. One-click reordering for favorite products.

Track order history, manage preferences, and view community contributions. Build your coffee journey timeline.
Brewing Guides
Access detailed brewing instructions for different methods optimized for each coffee variety to achieve the perfect cup.

Design & Technical Details

Design Approach

The design follows iOS design guidelines with a warm, coffee-inspired color palette dominated by rich browns and complementary neutral tones. Special attention was paid to creating an intuitive user experience that makes discovering and purchasing coffee products a delightful journey.

Design Principles

  • Warmth & Invitation: Colors and imagery evoke the comforting, inviting nature of coffee culture
  • Clarity & Simplicity: Clean layouts with focused content that guides users intuitively
  • Thoughtful Motion: Subtle animations that enhance the experience without distracting
  • Consistency: Unified visual language across all screens
  • Accessibility: Inclusive design choices ensuring the app is usable by people of all abilities

Technical Implementation

  • Responsive layouts that adapt to different screen sizes
  • Custom CSS variables for consistent styling
  • Semantic HTML structure for accessibility
  • CSS animations for interactive elements
  • Modular component design for scalability
