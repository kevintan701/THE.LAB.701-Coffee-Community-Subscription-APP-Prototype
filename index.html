THE.LAB.701 Coffee Community & Subscription App

Project Overview

A high-fidelity prototype for a digital coffee community and subscription app that connects coffee enthusiasts with premium coffee products and like-minded individuals. This app offers a seamless experience for discovering new coffee varieties, managing subscriptions, and engaging with a passionate coffee community.

Vision & Purpose

THE.LAB.701 Coffee Community app aims to revolutionize how coffee lovers discover, purchase, and experience specialty coffee. In a market saturated with generic coffee offerings, our platform creates a curated ecosystem where quality, sustainability, and community converge. We're not just selling coffee—we're building a digital third space where the rituals and pleasures of coffee can be shared and celebrated.

Key Features

  • Personalized Coffee Discovery: Browse and discover coffee products based on flavor profiles, origin, and roast levels. Our recommendation engine learns from your preferences to suggest new coffees that match your taste profile.
  • Flexible Subscription System: Customize delivery frequency (weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly), quantity, and grind options for regular coffee deliveries. Easily pause, skip, or modify your subscription anytime.
  • Community Engagement: Share brewing techniques, rate products, and connect with other coffee enthusiasts. Post your coffee experiences, follow other users, and participate in discussions about brewing methods, equipment, and coffee origins.
  • Seamless Ordering: Easy checkout process with saved payment methods and shipping addresses. One-click reordering for favorite products and subscription management.
  • User Profiles: Track order history, manage preferences, and view community contributions. Build your coffee journey timeline and showcase your tasting notes and favorite coffees.
  • Brewing Guides: Access detailed brewing instructions for different methods (pour-over, French press, espresso, etc.) optimized for each coffee variety to achieve the perfect cup.
  • Coffee Education: Learn about coffee origins, processing methods, and flavor characteristics through interactive content and storytelling elements that highlight the journey from farm to cup.

Target Audience

The app is designed for coffee enthusiasts across the spectrum—from curious beginners to seasoned connoisseurs. Primary user segments include:

  • Coffee Explorers: Individuals eager to expand their coffee palate beyond commercial offerings
  • Convenience Seekers: Busy professionals who value quality coffee delivered on their schedule
  • Community Builders: Social coffee lovers who enjoy sharing experiences and learning from others
  • Sustainability Advocates: Consumers concerned with ethical sourcing and environmental impact

Design Approach

The design follows iOS design guidelines with a warm, coffee-inspired color palette dominated by rich browns and complementary neutral tones. Special attention was paid to creating an intuitive user experience that makes discovering and purchasing coffee products a delightful journey.

Design principles that guided our work:

  • Warmth & Invitation: Colors and imagery evoke the comforting, inviting nature of coffee culture
  • Clarity & Simplicity: Clean layouts with focused content that guides users intuitively
  • Thoughtful Motion: Subtle animations that enhance the experience without distracting
  • Consistency: Unified visual language across all screens to build familiarity and ease of use
  • Accessibility: Inclusive design choices ensuring the app is usable by people of all abilities

Technical Implementation

This high-fidelity prototype is built using HTML, CSS, and minimal JavaScript to simulate the app experience. Key technical aspects include:

  • Responsive layouts that adapt to different screen sizes
  • Custom CSS variables for consistent styling
  • Semantic HTML structure for accessibility
  • CSS animations for interactive elements
  • Modular component design for scalability

The prototype showcases a complete user journey from onboarding to subscription management, with careful attention to intuitive navigation, responsive design, and delightful micro-interactions that enhance the overall coffee experience.

Future Development

While this prototype demonstrates core functionality, future development would include:

  • Backend integration with a robust e-commerce platform
  • User authentication and personalization features
  • Advanced recommendation algorithms
  • Real-time community features including live events and expert Q&A sessions
  • Augmented reality features for exploring coffee packaging and brewing setups
